Fizzbuzz!

Here is the classic problem of Fizzbuzz. All numbers between 1 and 100 are shown. If the number is divisible by 3, "Fizz!" is shown. If the number is divisible by 5, "Buzz!" is shown. If the number is divisible by both, then "Fizz Buzz! is shown."

On the left, we see the results of Fizzbuzz. On the right, we show each result, line by line as its calculated. Impressive!
